About Fintent

We design for
those who move
capital.

Fintent is a specialist design studio working exclusively within the financial sector — helping organisations articulate their value with rigour, restraint, and intention.

Built for finance.
Built with purpose.

Fintent was founded in 2026 with a single belief: that financial brands deserve design that matches their ambition. Too often, the sector settles for generic — safe palettes, forgettable logos, interfaces that obscure rather than clarify.

We exist to change that. Every engagement starts with deep sector knowledge and ends with work that earns the trust of clients, investors, and partners.

We are a small, senior team. No juniors doing the work while partners take the credit. When you work with Fintent, you work with the people who built our reputation.

What we stand for

01
Clarity over complexity

We believe great financial design simplifies without dumbing down. Complexity is a problem, not a feature.

02
Rigour at every step

We apply the same discipline to a typeface choice as to a brand architecture. Nothing is arbitrary.

03
Senior-only delivery

Every project is led and delivered by senior designers. The work you see in our portfolio is the work you get.
